 Creation of a new logo for Geant4 - Rules of the Competition

                                       

Article 1: Purpose of the competition

                                       
Geant4 collaboration is organizing a competition for the creation of its new logo.
                                       

Article 2: Participation

                                       
The competition is open to anyone, whether professional or amateur.
Participants under the age of 18 must have written parental consent to enter the competition.
                                       

Article 3: Duration of the competition

                                       
The competition is open from xxx to yyy 2016. Proposals must be received by the Geant4 logo contest teem no later than 23.59 p.m. on xxx 2016.
                                       

Article 4: Technical requirements

                                       
The new logo will be used on all means of communication of Geant4. The products must meet the following requirements:
Square size 150x150
Rectangle size (ratio 4:1) for banners, web site and publications papers
A slogan could be add in the horizontal shape
The resolution must be 300 dpi minimum,
The logo must support conversion to black and white and must remain legible when reduced to 200 x 200 pixels, – the fonts must be free of copyright and texts must be vectorized,
– accepted file types: psd, ai, eps, jpg, png...
– the participant’s name or signature must not be included in the logo.
                                       

Article 5: Conditions for participation

                                       
Participation is on an individual basis, free of charge and does not require prior registration.
Each participant may submit only one proposal by e-mail.
The e-mail must include the name, age, postal address, phone number and e-mail address of the participant.
The proposal must be accompanied by:
– a short description explaining the symbolism of the graphics used,
– a copy of these rules with your initials on the first page, and the text “Read and approved” with the date and your signature on the second page.
Any proposal received after the closing date or not meeting the requirements will be excluded from the competition.
                                       

Article 6: Copyright

                                       
Each participant submitting a proposal acknowledges he/she is the author of the logos and slogan (if created). The participant must also certify that the design of the logo not affect the rights of third parties and does not infringe copyright, directly or indirectly.
The winner undertakes to give for free the full copyrights to the Geant4 Collaboration. The winning logo will become the sole property of the Geant4 collaboration which reserves the right to use, modify, adapt and reproduce it on any media without time limit and without such use conferring the winner, or their family, any rights whatsoever other than the award of a prize.
                                       

Article 7: Jury

                                       
The jury is appointed by the Executive Committee. It is made up of seven members of the Staff Association, including communication professionals.
The jury is held to strict confidentiality as to the content of its discussions.
The jury's decisions are not subject to appeal or claim. No correspondence will be exchanged with the participants.
                                       
The jury reserves the right to make technical modifications to the design.
                                       

Article 8: Criteria for selection of finalists by the jury

                                       
Each logo will be examined according to the following criteria: a) representation of the values of the CERN staff Association, b) visual quality (graphics, colour, and font),
c) possibility to adapt it on different media,
                                       
d) originality of the design, e) legibility,
f) simplicity.
                                       

Article 9: Exhibition, selection of the finalists and designation of the winner

                                       
From 4 to 15 February 2013, the logos will be on display in the Main Building.
The jury will evaluate the proposals and will select the first three finalists within 15 days following the closing date of the competition. Three other finalists will be designated by CERN employed members of the personnel.
The six selected logos will be then submitted to the members of the CERN Staff Association for an electronic vote from 18 to 22 February 2013.
                                       

Article 10: Announcement of the results

                                       
The winner will be notified directly by the CERN Staff Association. The winning logo and the name of the winner will be published as from 25 February on the CERN Staff Association website and in Echo.
The winning logo will be adopted by the CERN staff Association for use in its communication and on its website.
                                       

Article 11: Prizes

                                       
The CERN Staff Association will award the following prizes:
1st Prize: an Ezee Suisse electric bike.
2nd Prize: vouchers for Go Sport with a value of 200 euros.
3rd Prize: vouchers for Go Sport with a value of 100 euros.
4th to 6th Prize: a voucher for Go Sport with a value of 50 euros.
                                       
Other prizes offered by our commercial partners (Aquaparc, BCGE, L’Occitane, Sephora, Theater La Comédie de Genève) will be drawn among the participants.
                                       

Article 12: Cancellation of the competition

                                       
If the jury considers there are not enough proposals or none is satisfactory, the CERN Staff Association reserves the right to postpone, suspend or cancel the competition at any time during the procedure. No claims from the candidates will be entertained.
                                       

Article 13: Exclusion

                                       
The Staff Association reserves the right to exclude from the competition any candidate having plagiarized the work of another candidate or having plagiarized a graphic element protected by copyright.
                                       

Article 14: Adherence to the rules

                                       
All candidates acknowledge having read these rules and accept all of its provisions, without exception. The participants undertake to:
– respect the rules of participation in the competition,
– allow the exhibition of their proposals to the public and on the CERN Staff Association website,
                                       
– transfer any copyrights to the CERN Staff Association which will become the owner of all the rights concerning the winning logo (the creator's name will not appear on the documents making use of the logo),
                                       
– not use or copy all or part of an existing logo.
