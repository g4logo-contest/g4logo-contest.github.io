Geant4 Logo Competition

   The Geant4 logo is already 20 years old! We are looking for a new, fresh logo which is eye-catching and also better defines what we do and who we are as a collaboration. It should also allow others to refer to us and our code on their web-pages (e.g. companies, universities, documentation, conferences...) as well as to identify us on video platforms (e.g. Youtube, Vimeo, Dailymotion) and social media (Facebook, Twitter, ...).
Put your creativity to work and send us your proposals.

Two vector formats are required :

  • A square logo, ratio 1:1 (for Youtube, Facebook, ...)
  • A horizontal banner, ratio 4:1 (for web-pages and publications)
  • A slogan (or design space) could be add in the horizontal shape (max 40 characters)

Contest Details


Project information

Geant4 is a toolkit for the simulation of the passage of particles through matter. Its areas of application include high energy, nuclear and accelerator physics, as well as studies in medical and space science.
The areas of application include high energy, nuclear and accelerator physics, as well as studies in medical and space science The software is used by tens of thousands of people - scientists researchers, students and engineers. The project began 22 years ago at CERN, but is a world-wide collaboration involving many nationalities. The logo should reflect both the scientific nature of particle physics detector simulation and the collaborative nature of our project. The associated publicity can be far reaching.
We are willing to acknowledge the winning designer on our homepage.

Web site : https://geant4.web.cern.ch/geant4/


Who can submit a logo?

The competition is open to anyone, whether professional or amateur.

Deadline

The contest is open for one week, from 28th October 2016 17:00 pm (CEST) until 11th November 2016 17:00 pm (CEST).

Selection for the winner

All entries will be screened and those that comply with the Terms and conditions and have met the guidelines and specifications, as seen on this page, will be judged by the Geant4 collaboration members by a vote.

The winner will be announced on November 21th and will be notified email. In the event that no entry is selected, Geant4 reserves the right to declare no winner and run the contest again at a later date.
